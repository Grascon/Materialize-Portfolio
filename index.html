Hello! My Name is Gilberto Rascon and I am 25 years old. I was born and raised in Tucson, AZ. My parents are from Mexico and I speak both English and Spanish. I graduated from the University of Arizona in 2014 with a B.A in Anthropology and have been working at non-profits for the last 3 years. I have mostly worked with children and immigrant populations from Central America.

Recently I decided to pursue a career change and decided to enroll into the U of A Coding Boot Camp to hopefully transition into a web developer career. My goal at the moment is to finish the program and hopefully gain employment as a front-end developer.

Besides that, my hobbies include spending time with my girlfriend, family, dog, and going to the movies. I also like soccer and my favorite teams are Arsenal F.C., F.C. Barcelona, and the Men's Mexican National Team.
